Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What we collect

MeetingPoint collects only the minimum personal information required to operate the service: your name, email address, and any optional profile information you choose to provide (bio, location, interests, profile photo).

How we use it

We use your information to provide and improve the platform, send event-related notifications and reminders you have opted into, and to power personalized recommendations based on your activity such as events you join or bookmark.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by applicable law. We do not serve advertisements.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You can control the visibility of your profile and event history at any time from your profile settings.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in, and a cookie to remember your consent to this policy. No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies are used.

Data security

All passwords are hashed using Bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Sessions are protected with secure, HTTP-only cookies. All forms are protected against CSRF attacks.
